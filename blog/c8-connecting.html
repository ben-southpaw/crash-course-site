Political Awareness Exercise

The situation I refer to is about a former flatmate who I felt abused the trust of the others and acted in a somehwat selfish manner across many areas. He often neglected to do his part in terms of cleaning, contributing a fair share to bills and was socially non-involved most of the time. I tried to foster good relations through offering invites to events, to cook him food or donate things I had, which he might appreciate. All of this was, in my view, taken advantage of and unappreciated.

From the perspective of the flatmate I can see someone thinking that if no one explicitly states there is a problem there is no problem. Also if I have a poor memory how am I to blame for forgetting simple things, my intentions were to do the right thing. Lastly, people shouldn't expect anything in return for what they offer as thats not a genuine offer really!
